Our Story

Where it all began

Journalism-based. Data-driven. Creative-focused.

With roots in Europe, the Americas, and Asia, our international team of journalists, marketers & creatives have helped Nordic brands reach global audiences for over two decades.

We came together out of shared respect and great experiences working on projects for our long-standing clients. Together, we jointly bring nearly 100 years of award-winning experience in journalism, marketing, and communications to our client work.

Today, Aspidistra stands tall as a journalism-powered, strategic & creative agency, based in the heart of Helsinki, Finland. Our strategy, content production, and skills development services ensure our clients keep their brand flying.
